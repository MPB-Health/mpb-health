✅ Required Documents Checklist

Itemized medical bills from provider (showing CPT codes and detailed charges)
Proof of payment (receipts, credit card statements, or EOB)
Medical records or visit notes (for major procedures)
Pre-authorization documentation (if required for procedure)
Prescription documentation (if claiming pharmacy expenses)
Lab/imaging results (if applicable)
Completed MPB Health sharing request form

💡 Important Tips

  • Always Request Itemized Bills: Ask providers for detailed, itemized bills showing procedure codes
  • Negotiate Costs: Ask about cash discounts or payment plans before paying
  • Track Pre-Authorization: For major procedures over $5,000, contact MPB Health for pre-notification
  • Keep Digital Copies: Scan or photograph all documents for your records
  • Submit Within 180 Days: Submit bills within 180 days of service for timely processing
  • Know Your IUA: Remember your Initial Unshareable Amount ($1,250, $2,500, or $5,000)
