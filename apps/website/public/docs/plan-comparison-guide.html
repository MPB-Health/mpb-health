Important Notes

  • *Pricing: Monthly contributions shown are for Member Only, ages 18-29, with $1,250 IUA. Actual rates vary by age, household size, and IUA selection. Visit mpb.health/get-started for a personalized quote.
  • Initial Unshareable Amount (IUA): This is the amount you pay before sharing begins for each medical need. Think of it like a deductible.
  • Pre-existing Conditions: Conditions diagnosed or treated in the 24 months prior to membership have a waiting period before they become shareable.
  • Maternity: Conception must occur 6+ months after membership effective date for maternity expenses to be shareable.
  • Not Insurance: Health sharing is not insurance. Members voluntarily share each other's medical expenses.
